Trade

My areas of business interests include power generation equipments because this was the area of my studies at university, and later on my business interests have enlarged to encompass also the business aviation industry, yachts, real estates, IT etc. I will continue with these businesses whenever there is a chance.
 

There are a number of possibilities for you and me to do trade between your country and China or anywhere I might be settled down.

I can find the suppliers in China for you and give you the contact details so that you can negotiate with them yourself, and you pay a little money for the information, or we can negotiate with the suppliers on behalf of you, of course you also need to pay a little for our services as your negotiator.
