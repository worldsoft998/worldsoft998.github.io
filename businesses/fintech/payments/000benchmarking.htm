Online Payment Benchmarking
Consumers need to know which payment systems to use when paying online. This is very important to ensure your online payment is secured, guaranteed and without losses. Here we compile some of the key online payment system rankings made available to you.
These rankings include Worldwide Payment Top 10, and some of the Regional Rankings, according to their general reputations we obtain from the cosumer surveys. There are also some particular considerations when making such rankings, such as their fees, speedness of processing money transfer, easiness of registering and payment, availability of their payment interface, number of currencies and banks they support, languages they can handle, countries where they are operating, and of course reliability of their services. All these and others contribute to the final decision of the rankings.
