Online Payment Provider Directory eBook

This ebook lists alphabetically about 500 online payment providers / gateways, worldwide, with some of the key information such as:

  • company name,
  • date founded,
  • key personnel,
  • capital and revenues,
  • staff number,
  • geographical locations of business,
  • types of business,
  • websites and contacts etc

The list is updated quarterly and is available upon a small payment. The list is in PDF and can also be customized according to client's needs.
