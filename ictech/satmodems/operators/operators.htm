Operators

Satellite broadband Internet operators, solution, service and engineering companies and others.
 

Here we've divided the business sector into three (3) parts (tiers):

Tier One - those with their own satellites on orbit

Tier Two - those helping Tier One to win subscribers, that is, the service providers on the ground and

Tier Three - all those providing engineering, consulting and technical expertise for Tier One and Tier Two and also including clients.

And last the China part, where you have some information from the Chinese industry.
