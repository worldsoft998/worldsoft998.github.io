Communications - General
 

The design of satellite broadband Internet modem IC cores involve lots of technologies, of course also the communications technologies, both wired and wireless and radio communications. For years I've been studying the fundamentals of these technologies and thus have gained a global picture of the systems, key components, key technologies, key mathematics (mainly Fourier transformation), but I am far from being an expert here yet, therefore I need to continue the studies of this field of science during my lifetime, to keep myself current with the new technologies.
 
My current reading or repeat reading (for most of the literature I have in my computer are already read over the last years)
 

Grami2016 Introduction to Digital Communications p587

CHAPTER 9
Information Theory
