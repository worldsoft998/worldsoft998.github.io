Satellite Broadband Internet

Land-based Internet imposes great challenges to people with great mobility or least freedom.

On one hand, those who move around the globe very often will find it very annoying to switch from one ISP to another, and on the other hand, despotic government can restrict people in those countries in their access to free information from the Internet, when the only option for them is a wired Internet access.

Satellite comes in to solve the above two problems.
