Secure Web Services

 

Are you aware of the risks when exposed to the Internet? Are you being sniffed during your online time? Are you worrying about the disclosure of your sensitive data and information while surfing the Web, making online business, making online orders and payments?

Many people don't. It's actually a bad news to them. They simply don't realize how dangerous they are when connected to the Internet. Virtually all your communications are not secure, vulnerable to attacks whether browsing websites, sending or receiving mails, chatting in IMs, making file uploads or downloads, and online telephony via VoIP etc. All your messages, texts, voices and pictures, when they are not properly protected, are in clear forms and might be captured by the men-in-the-middle.

The Internet now is becoming more and more unsafe, restrictive and fragile to blocking, filtering and attacks. As a results, netizens are enjoying less and less freedom and are worrying about the normal operation of their Internet access, their own computer and their confidence in the privacy, liberty and security when connected to the World Wide Web.

Now, the good news is that all over the world many people are working around ways, technologies and systems to get rid of these troubles. As a result of these efforts, many technologies are available which are able to solve the above problems. We have studied these intensively over the last couple of years and have worked out a solution package called SecureWeb which might be the right choice for you. It is expected to guarantee your online security while exchanging data and information over the Internet with proper encryption/ciphering, whether for browsing and surfing, sending and receiving emails, conducting instant messaging or chatting, making VoIP calls, and any other data transfer over the Internet. This service package is customized for your specific needs, and is modified according to your online environment along with the time.
